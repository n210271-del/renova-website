How Renova Works

  1. Collect & segregate community waste at source.
  2. Process in modular anaerobic digestion / gasification units.
  3. Recover energy (kWh) for municipal loads and export to grid.
  4. Convert residuals into by-products for construction and agriculture.

IoT sensors monitor temperature, gas output, and equipment health — enabling predictive maintenance and transparent reporting for municipal stakeholders.

By-Products from Waste-to-Energy

Beyond electricity, Renova recovers materials that feed local economies and promote circular construction practices.

EB

Eco-Bricks

Stabilized bottom ash transformed into lightweight, durable bricks for non-structural construction and landscaping.

BF

Biofertilizer

Nutrient-rich organic fertilizer produced from organic digestate — supports urban agriculture and green spaces.

BC

Biochar

Carbon-rich material enhancing soil health, water retention, and serving as a carbon sequestration method.

SA

Synthetic Aggregates

Processed ash and slag repurposed as aggregates for road base, paver blocks, and concrete mixes.

CA

Construction Ash

Fly ash and processed residues used as cement additives and fillers in pavers and blocks.

RDF

RDF (Refuse Derived Fuel)

Processed combustible fractions used as alternative fuel in industrial kilns, reducing fossil fuel use.
